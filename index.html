Tank Game

Instructions for the game:

  • Move mouse to control the turret.
  • Use left and right arrow keys to turn the tank.
  • Use up and down arrow keys to move forwards and backwards.
  • Use left click to shoot. You get points when you hit enemies.
  • Collect powerups to gain special effects (speed up, piercing shot, invincibility, health).
  • Watch out for the red enemy tanks!
  • Try to survive and get as much points as you can!
